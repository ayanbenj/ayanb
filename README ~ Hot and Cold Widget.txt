Hot and Cold Widget

How does it work?
Input a number in the Temperature fo Food box
Choose which meat item would you like to see the right temperature or 
the reduced temperature

All numbers for temperatures are in Farenheit

This widget is to receive the temperature of a fridge or freezer, 
including what type of meat a person is trying to cool or keep frozen, 
and know what is the ideal temperature for cooling or freezing a certain 
type of meat.
